## Key Rules for App Store Keywords

- **Title (30 chars):** Your #1 keyword with brand (e.g., "Merge Items" or "Deep Work Focus Timer")
- **Subtitle (30 chars):** Your #2 keyword describing key feature
- **Keyword field (100 chars):** Comma-separated, unique words (no spaces after commas, no repeats from title/subtitle)
- **Target zone:** Difficulty < 35, SP 35-55 for new apps
- **Refresh:** Every 4-6 weeks, swap out underperforming keywords
- **Long-tail:** Lower volume but 2-3x higher conversion rate
