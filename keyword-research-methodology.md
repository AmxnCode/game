# How to Find Good App Store Keywords — Full Methodology + Results

> Applied to: Mobile Games (Merge/Puzzle) & AI Productivity Tools
> Research date: June 2026

---

## The 7-Step Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Write 3-5 core phrases describing your app in user language (not technical jargon).

### Step 2: Autocomplete Mining
Open App Store → type each seed + each letter of alphabet (a-z) → record all suggestions. These are REAL user searches.

### Step 3: Competitor Gap Analysis
Find 3-5 competitor apps. Look at keywords they rank for in positions 5-20 (not 1-3). These are terms they're NOT fully optimized for — you can steal them.

### Step 4: Modifier Combinations
Append modifiers to your seeds: `best`, `free`, `simple`, `daily`, `offline`, `for beginners`, `iphone`, `3d`.

### Step 5: Score on Volume & Difficulty
- **Apple Search Popularity (SP):** 35-55 = meaningful volume (~2,200-8,200 daily impressions)
- **Difficulty (KD):** < 35 = winnable for new apps
- **KOB Score:** (Volume / Difficulty) × 10 — higher = better

### Step 6: Prioritize via Opportunity Matrix

| | Low Difficulty (< 35) | High Difficulty (> 35) |
|---|---|---|
| **High Volume (SP 35+)** | **PRIMARY** — title & subtitle | Avoid for new apps |
| **Low Volume (SP < 35)** | Keyword field filler | Skip entirely |

### Step 7: Assign to Metadata
- **Title (30 chars):** Your #1 keyword (best opportunity)
- **Subtitle (30 chars):** Your #2 keyword
- **Keyword field (100 chars):** Comma-separated unique words, no repeats from title/subtitle, no spaces after commas

Refresh every 4-6 weeks — swap out underperformers.

## How to Continue This Yourself (Without Paid Tools)

1. **Open App Store on your phone** → Type your seed keyword → write down all autocomplete suggestions
2. **Do the alphabet trick:** Type `merge a`, `merge b`, `merge c`... all 26 letters → record all suggestions
3. **Check competitors:** Search your keyword → look at top 10 apps → note their titles and subtitles → find keywords they're NOT using
4. **Modifier test:** Add words like `free`, `best`, `simple`, `3d`, `offline` to your seeds → note which combos autocomplete
5. **Prioritize:** Pick terms that autocomplete early (high volume) + have smaller apps ranking (not just giants)
6. **Test:** Put your best 8-12 keywords into metadata → wait 2 weeks → check if you're ranking → double down on winners, swap losers
